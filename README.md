 Overview:
The Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence report designed to analyze and visualize key sales metrics across multiple coffee shop branches and product categories. It provides real-time insights into revenue, profit, transaction volume, and product performance across different cities and shop locations.
This dashboard helps stakeholders quickly identify top-performing branches, track sales trends, and make data-driven decisions to improve operations and profitability.
Objectives :
To monitor total sales performance across multiple branches and cities.
To analyze revenue and profit trends over time.
To understand product category performance and contribution to total sales.
To identify top-performing shops and cities.
To support strategic decision-making for marketing, inventory, and expansion planning
